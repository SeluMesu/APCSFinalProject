Beneficial Effect

A beneficial effect is that facial recognition enhances safety and security. Organizations or government can use it to identify and track any person who is not allowed. Organizations can easily flag any visitors who are not welcomed. Government can use it to monitor people coming in and going in airports for such as people who have overstayed their visas or may be under criminal investigation.

Harmful Effect

A harmful effect is that the video or image need to be a high quality and high quality sensors and cameras is need. To store high quality videos and images takes significant amount of storage and process to be able to identify effectively. They have to invest a lot of money on this technology to be use it.
