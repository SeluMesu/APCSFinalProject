Data Using

Facial Recognition consumes image from from image, video or real person. The image that is consumed, an algorithm reads the geometer of face such as forehead, chin, nose, distance of eyes. The algorithm identifies facial landmarks — the system identifies 68 of them — that are key to distinguishing your face. The geometry form the image compares with database faces geometry. The algorithm determine and shows the match it found.

Privacy Concern

A privacy concern is that to increase the accuracy of facial recognition companies collect customers data. Customers do not have access to thiers data and don’t know what companies could used it for. For instance Amazon launches grocery store that doesn't use human cashier. Everything is done using facial recognition. Amazon can use customers data to sale products.
